The Post-Graduation Diploma in Yoga Therapy [PGDyT] is aimed at producing eminent holistic Yoga Therapists. Yoga Therapy uses the ancient science of yoga to enhance the health and wellness at all levels of the individual physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual. Wide range of ailments/diseases such as diabetes, blood pressure, anxiety, depression, back pain, sinusitis, heart related issues and many more can be successfully treated. Yoga therapy respects individual differences in age, culture, religion, philosophy, occupation, mental and physical health. Yoga Therapy increases the ability of an individual to utilize the medicine. There are five sheaths which encase the body such as annamaya kosha, pranamaya kosha, manomaya kosha, vijnanamaya Kosha and anandamaya Kosha. Yoga Therapy works positively at all the five dimensions or koshas.



Note:

  • There shall be 2 compulsary contact programmes for the candidates.
  • There shall be an examination at the end of the course.
  • _____________________________________________________

    • The main objective of Post-Graduation Diploma in Yoga Therapy is to equip students to understand the ailments and design and develop a customized Yoga Therapy Module and apply the same to enhance the healing process. The PGDyT course covers the subjects of philosophy, history and development of yogic science. Course incorporates the study and practice of asanas, pranayama, meditation, mudras, bandhas and kriyas. PGDyT includes study of anatomy and physiology of the human body. Course also covers study of lifestyle changes and dietary practices to enhance the healing process.
